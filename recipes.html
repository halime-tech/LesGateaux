Classic Vanilla Cake

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup milk
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Mix all dry ingredients in a bowl.
  3. Add eggs, milk, and vanilla extract, then mix well.
  4. Pour into a greased cake pan and bake for 35 minutes.
  5. Let cool before serving.
