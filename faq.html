Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of the most common questions our clients ask about Les Gâteaux.

Do you deliver cakes to other cities?

Yes, we offer nationwide delivery. Delivery fees may vary depending on your location.

Can I customize my cake design?

Absolutely! You can send us a photo or describe your idea, and our chefs will create it for you.

How far in advance should I order my cake?

We recommend placing your order at least 2–3 days in advance to ensure availability and freshness.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, mobile money, and bank transfers. Online payment integration is coming soon!
